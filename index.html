Abstract

I am a final-year CS PhD at UCLA, Founder and Lead of TurningPoint AI Research, and affiliated with Google Research / DeepMind. I was the 1st-author recepient of the Outstanding Paper Award at ICLR. I obtained my B.S. in Computer Science and Statistics from University of Michigan, with the highest distinction. I study Multimodal Foundational Models, and am into Venture Capitals.
