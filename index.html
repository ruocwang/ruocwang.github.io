About Me

I am currently a Ph.D. student at UCLA, advised by Prof. Cho-Jui Hsieh (opens new window). My research area is Efficient and Robust Deep Learning method. For the past two years, I mainly focus on AutoML (NAS, HPO, Dataset) and scalable GNN.

I obtained my B.S. degree (dual) in Computer Science and Statistics at University of Michigan - Ann Arbor, with highest distinction. During this period, I interned at MRSA and Sensetime on machine learning and computer vision, as well as helped a startup to develop its prototype robots.
